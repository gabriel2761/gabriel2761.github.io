Sydney Guide

In 2021, SBIR and NSW Transport asked developers to find a way to help people navigate the train stations.

Visually impaired people needed to be greatly considered.

I came up with an app that was able to recognise the unique signs and architecture of Sydney's train stations.

It could pin point you on a map and provide the you with real-time information.

Unfortunately my solution wasn't chosen.

I was told that other teams, had more experience with commercialization.

They wanted to make money off it.

I have so far discarded to project as it takes many hours to create the machine learning models for each sign.

I did have a solution to make a model with just one image.

Maybe in the future when I have the time and resources I'll just make the app to help visually impaired people for free.
